$$ \tilde{J}(\mathbf{\theta};\mathbf{X},\mathbf{y}) = J(\mathbf{\theta}; \mathbf{X}, \mathbf{y}) + \alpha\Omega(\mathbf{\theta}) \text{, } \alpha \in[0,\infty) $$
  • $J$ : standard objective function
  • $\tilde{J}$ : regularizedobjective function
  • $\mathbf{\theta}$ : Weight
  • $ \alpha $ : Norm contribution
