consider a regression task

$$ J=\mathbb{E}_{p({x},y)}[(\hat{y}({x}) - y)^2] $$

now assume that with each input presentation we also include a random perturbation: $ \epsilon_W\sim\mathcal{N}(\epsilon; 0, \eta I) $ Minimization of $$ \tilde{J}=\mathbb{E}_{p({x},y_{\epsilon_w}, \epsilon_W)}[(\hat{y}({x}) - y)^2] $$ is equivalent to minimization of $J$ with additional term: $$ \eta\mathbb{E}_p(x,y)[\| \nabla w\hat{y}(x) \|^2] $$
