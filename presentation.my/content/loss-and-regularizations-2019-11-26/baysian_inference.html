$$ P(H|E) = \frac{P(E|H)\cdot P(H)}{P(E)} $$ \begin{align} p(\theta \mid \mathbf {X} ,\alpha )&={\frac {p(\theta ,\mathbf {X} ,\alpha )}{p(\mathbf {X} ,\alpha )}}={\frac {p(\mathbf {X} \mid \theta ,\alpha )p(\theta ,\alpha )}{p(\mathbf {X} \mid \alpha )p(\alpha )}}={\frac {p(\mathbf {X} \mid \theta ,\alpha )p(\theta \mid \alpha )}{p(\mathbf {X} \mid \alpha )}} \\\\ &\propto p(\mathbf {X} \mid \theta ,\alpha )p(\theta \mid \alpha ) \end{align}
  • $H$ : hypothesis
  • $E$ : evidence
