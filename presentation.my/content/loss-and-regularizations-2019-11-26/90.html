mistakes in labels is harmful to maximize $\log p(y|x)$.

assume with a small constant $\epsilon$, label $y$ is correct, we can regularize a model by change the target:

  • $0 \rightarrow \frac{\epsilon}{k-1} $
  • $1 \rightarrow 1-\epsilon$

Label smoothing prevent the output becoming too large. Similar to weight decay.
